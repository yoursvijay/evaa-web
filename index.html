Core Features

Why do you need an AI-drive touchless digital receptionist?
100% Contactless Check-in Experience

A complete touchless hyper-intuitive interface for safe & express check-in/check-out activities

Temperature Detection

Automatic and non-invasive temperature monitoring powered by advanced sensors & lenses, allowing only those with safe temperature to enter your premises

Face & Mask Detection

AI-powered face detection technology that instantly detects if the visitor is wearing COVID-19 safety masks, ensuring higher safety for your business

Hand & Bag Sanitization

In-built sensor-enabled hand-sanitization pod & bag-sanitization hub that adheres to highest safety standards meant for the current times.

Additional Features

Peerless front-desk optimizations opening vistas for growth
Sanitize your bags & accessories

Touch-free sanitizing chamber for your bags and accessories which means every visitor that enters your building carries germs from outside.

Visual & LED Indicators

That give clear cut signals if a visitor is permitted to enter or not, making the whole check-in experience automated, swift, & reliable

Admin Dashboard

Evaa can be dynamically accessed by the admin, providing accurate and detailed information, keeping the admin up-to-date with every bit of useful visitor information

Live Streaming

With Evaa, you can enable the live view & streaming through the admin panel which lets the admin get a complete view of the front-desk activities, live, for every visitor.

Live Video Call with Admin

Once the temperature check has been carried out and it indicates a safe level, the app will directly place a video call between the visitor and the admin so that the access can be granted by the admin.

Speech Recognition

Evaa is equipped with leading speech recognition technology that enables back and forth conversations between Evaa & your visitors, adding a core human touch to the digital frontdesk automation.

Push Notifications

SMS, email reminders, & push notifications sent routinely to the admin & the visitors, based on the frontdesk activity, keeping every involved party well-informed about the visits thus alleviating physical security risks.

Custom Branding

Customize Evaa by adding your company name and logo on the tablet & admin module & make it an inherent entity of your business. Evaa will take the shape & identity of your brand while digitizing the complete front desk operations

256bit Encryption

256 bit encrypted security & GDPR compliant, ensuring the highest level of data security for your visitors data, meaning their personal data is safeguarded from cyberattacks, hackers, & other malicious threats.

Data on Cloud

Cloud-based storage of data which means more savings, better data accessibility, and higher data security for all the visitors to your organization premises
